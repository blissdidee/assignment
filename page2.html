Autobiography of a Cheff

Daniel Uggy

Connecting Naija to their Root

Here's a time line of Ngoka Daniel's life

  • 1980 - Born into the family of Sir and Lady Alfred of Owerri Nchise
  • 1986 - Started Primary Education
  • 1992 - Gained First School Leaving Certificate and proceeded to Keke High School
  • 1992 - Gained Senior School certificate fro K High School with outstanding distinctions
  • 2000 - Gained admission into the Federal University Of Technology to Study Cooking
  • 2009 - Completed First Degree and proceeded for National Youth service corp where he served his nation as a teacher of physics and mathematics in Benue state Oshongo LGA
  • 2011 - After 2 years of completing NYSC, jobs are not forth coming so he ventured into small Resturant business, selling food to make ends meet.
  • 2012 - Got a governmet job as a secretary to add to the growing business
  • 2013 - While marketing his food to bank officials and also looking for better opportunity, he made bankers his friends who helped upload his CV for possible recruitment
  • 2014 - Started his career as a banker and grew to assist branch service manager
  • 2016 - Got married to my beautiful wife
  • 2020 - Relocated to the United State for a better life and contyinued with the Resturant business.
  • 2021 - Got his certification as a Salesforce Administrator and in his love for salesforce, he proceeded to getting cdertified as a salesforce developer.

We counted it all joy, The reality is that the whole struggle of new and humble beginning started all over again but with strength and faith I believe I will over come as I have done in time past
